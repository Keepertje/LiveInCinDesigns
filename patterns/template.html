Chippie

Level:
Easy

Description:
Chippie is made with Scheepjes Stonewashed XL using a 4mm hook. This adorable mouse is 25 cm tall. Don’t forget to close your fridge after finishing Chippie, otherwise your cheese will be gone in a moment ;).
